Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

We believe privacy is a fundamental right — not a feature toggle. This policy explains what Benzinlik collects, why, and how we protect it. Short version: an e-mail, a hashed password, and your game save. Nothing else.

1. Who we are

Benzinlik is a free browser game available at petrol.benerits.com. It is a Benerits product, developed and operated by Hopsule Inc. ("we," "us," or "our").

2. Data we collect

  • Account information: your e-mail address and a password. Passwords are stored only as salted scrypt hashes — we cannot read them.
  • Game save data: your in-game progress (money, buildings, statistics, station name and similar gameplay values), stored as structured data linked to your account.
  • Technical logs: standard server logs (IP address, timestamps, request paths) kept briefly for security, rate limiting and abuse prevention.

What we do not collect: no name, no profile, no payment data, no contacts, no location, no device fingerprinting, no advertising identifiers.

3. How we use your data

  • Authenticate you and sync your game save across sessions and devices
  • Operate anti-cheat checks on submitted save data
  • Protect the Service against abuse (rate limiting, security monitoring)
  • Analyze aggregate, non-identifying gameplay statistics to improve the Game
  • Send transactional service e-mails and occasional announcements about Benzinlik and other Benerits products (such as new game releases) — you can opt out of announcements at any time

4. What we never do

  • We never sell, rent, or trade your personal data
  • We never show ads or share data with advertising networks
  • We never use your data to train AI models
  • We never share your data with third parties except as required to host the Service, deliver e-mails we send you, or comply with the law

5. Data storage & security

  • Your data lives in an isolated database dedicated solely to Benzinlik
  • All traffic between your browser and our servers uses TLS (HTTPS)
  • Passwords are salted and hashed with scrypt; session tokens are signed (HMAC) and expire automatically

6. Cookies & local storage

Benzinlik uses no cookies and no cross-site tracking. Your browser's local storage holds only your session token and your in-game preferences (such as sound settings). Clearing your browser data signs you out.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to object to specific processing. You can reset your game save at any time from the in-game settings, and opt out of announcement e-mails via the unsubscribe link or by contacting us. For account deletion or a copy of your data, contact us via hopsule.com — we will respond within 30 days.

8. Children

Benzinlik is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above and, where practical, announced in-game. Continued use of the Service after the effective date constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

Benzinlik is operated by Hopsule Inc. (Delaware, United States). For any privacy question or request, reach us via hopsule.com. See also our Terms of Service.
